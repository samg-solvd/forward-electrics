Who We Are
Company Overview

Forward Electrics is a reliable provider of electrical services and repairs, based in New South Wales, with a strong focus on the defence and transport industries. Founded in 2000 by Darren Forward, the company began as a family-owned business dedicated to providing specialised electrical services to the Australian Defence Force.


Since then it has grown to serve Sydney's freshwater class ferries and luxury private vessels. With a reputation built on reliability and expertise, Forward Electrics has become a trusted partner for a wide range of electrical requirements.

What We Do
Our Services

Specialised electrical solutions for marine, defence and commercial environments.

Electrical Repair

Reliable electrical solutions since 2000. Registered Thales vendor. Specialising in installation, troubleshooting, overhaul and repair of marine electrical and electronic systems. Services include power and lighting, auxiliary propulsion control, steering control, navigation aids, CCTV and more.

Navigation Aids

Extensive knowledge of radar, EOSS, Gyro and DVRS systems. CCTV installation and troubleshooting capability. Trusted by the Australian Navy for mission-critical navigation support.

Defence Work

Supporting the Australian Navy 24/7. Qualified technicians available around the clock for repairs, breakdowns and overhauls. Trusted subcontractor to the defence sector with a resolute commitment to top-tier service. Proximity to HMAS Waterhen and HMAS Kattabul.

Proven Results
Case Study: Auxiliary Propulsion Repair for Navy

Forward Electrics was tasked by the Australian Navy to repair an Auxiliary Propulsion Control System causing operational disruptions. Our engineers pinpointed the root cause — an outdated power supply causing intermittent failures — and replaced it with a more robust model. The system was recalibrated to Navy operational standards, tested under multiple conditions, and fully restored, ensuring the vessel's ongoing readiness.
